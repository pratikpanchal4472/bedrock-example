SHOPEASE CUSTOMER POLICY DOCUMENT

RETURNS AND REFUNDS
ShopEasy accepts returns within 30 days of delivery for most items.
Electronics must be returned within 7 days and must be unopened.
Clothing and footwear can be returned within 30 days if unworn and in original packaging.
To initiate a return, go to My Orders, select the item, and click Return Item.
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received.
Cash on delivery orders are refunded via bank transfer within 7-10 business days.

SHIPPING POLICY
Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days across India.
Express delivery takes 1-2 business days and is available in metro cities.
Free shipping applies to all orders above Rs 499.
Orders below Rs 499 incur a flat shipping fee of Rs 49.
ShopEasy ships to all states and union territories in India.

CANCELLATION POLICY
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement at no charge.
After 1 hour, cancellation is only possible if the order has not been shipped.
Once shipped, the item must be returned after delivery.
Prepaid orders that are cancelled are refunded within 3-5 business days.

WARRANTY POLICY
Electronics purchased on ShopEasy come with a standard 1-year manufacturer warranty.
Warranty claims must be initiated within the warranty period.
Physical damage is not covered under warranty.
Contact ShopEasy support with your order ID to initiate a warranty claim.
